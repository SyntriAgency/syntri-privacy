Privacy Policy – Syntri QR Scanner

Syntri QR Scanner respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Data Collection

  • Email address – collected via Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication).
  • Device identifiers – used for subscription and device management.
  • Subscription data – stored in Google Play Billing and Firebase Firestore.

Data Usage

Data is used only to provide app features: subscription management, device limits, and account authentication. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Data Security

All data is transmitted securely over HTTPS and stored with industry-standard protection.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account data (email, subscription info, device identifiers) at any time. Send your request to: syntri.business.agency@gmail.com. All requests are processed within 30 days.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at: syntri.business.agency@gmail.com.
