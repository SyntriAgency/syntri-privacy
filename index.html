Privacy Policy – Syntri QR Scanner

Syntri QR Scanner respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Data Collection

  • Email address – collected via Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication).
  • Device identifiers – used for subscription and device management.
  • Subscription data – stored in Google Play Billing and Firebase Firestore.

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to provide app functionality, including subscription management, device limits, and secure authentication. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Data Security

All data is transmitted securely over HTTPS and stored using industry-standard encryption and access control.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account data (email, subscription info, and device identifiers) at any time. Please send your request to: syntri.business.agency@gmail.com. All requests are processed within 30 days.

Contact
